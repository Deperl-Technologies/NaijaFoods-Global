Our Story

Born from a longing for home — NaijaFoods Global was founded by Nigerians in the diaspora who believed every family deserves the authentic taste of Nigeria, no matter where they live.

Who We Are

From a Kitchen in Lagos to Kitchens Worldwide

NaijaFoods Global started in 2019 when our founder, Adaeze Okafor, moved to Houston, Texas and couldn't find authentic Nigerian ingredients anywhere nearby. What began as care packages from family back home evolved into a mission to serve the entire Nigerian diaspora.

Today, we source directly from trusted farms and suppliers across Nigeria, carefully curating and packaging the finest grains, spices, sauces, and ready-to-cook meal kits — delivering them fresh to over 45 countries worldwide.

"Every package we send is a piece of home. We don't just deliver food — we deliver memories, traditions, and love."

— Adaeze Okafor, Founder & CEO
Our Journey

How We Got Here

A timeline of passion, growth, and thousands of happy families served.

2019 — The Spark

A Care Package From Lagos

Our founder received her first "food package" from her mother in Lagos. She realized millions of Nigerians abroad share the same craving for authentic ingredients.

2020 — The Launch

First 50 Orders Shipped

Operating from a small warehouse in Houston, we shipped our first 50 orders to families across the US. Word spread through WhatsApp groups and community pages.

2022 — Going Global

Expanded to UK, Canada & Europe

With growing demand, we opened distribution in London and Toronto, serving 15+ countries. Our meal kit concept was born — making cooking easier for busy diaspora families.

2024 — The Milestone

50,000 Families Served

We reached 50,000 happy families across 45+ countries. Launched our premium spice line and partnered with Nigerian farmers for direct sourcing.

2026 — Today

Building the Future

380+ products, same-week delivery in key markets, and a community of food lovers who keep our Nigerian culinary heritage alive worldwide.

Home/Our Mission

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria with every family in the diaspora — making authentic food accessible, affordable, and joyful to prepare.

What Drives Us

Three Pillars of Our Mission

Everything we do is guided by these core commitments to our community, our heritage, and our future.

Preserve Heritage

Nigerian cuisine is one of the world's richest culinary traditions. We ensure that recipes, techniques, and flavors are passed down to the next generation — even thousands of miles from home.

Empower Communities

We partner directly with Nigerian farmers and local producers, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. Every order you place supports livelihoods back home.

Connect the Diaspora

Food is culture. Food is identity. We build bridges between Nigerians worldwide, creating a global community united by the shared love of authentic home cooking.

Our Vision

A World Where Distance Doesn't Dilute Tradition

We envision a future where every Nigerian family — whether in Lagos, London, or Los Angeles — can enjoy the same authentic flavors. Where a child born in the diaspora knows the taste of their grandmother's Jollof Rice.

Our long-term vision extends beyond commerce. We're building an ecosystem that includes recipe sharing, cooking classes, cultural events, and a community platform that celebrates Nigerian food culture globally.

  • Partner with 100+ Nigerian farms by 2028
  • Reach families in 80+ countries by 2030
  • Launch free online Nigerian cooking academy
  • Establish NaijaFoods Foundation for food education

Home/FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, and our products.

Ordering
How do I place an order?
Simply browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay with all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. We'll send a confirmation email with your order details and tracking number once shipped.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, orders enter our packing process and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email if you need to make changes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We also offer Klarna for buy-now-pay-later options on orders over $50.
Shipping
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days for US and UK, 7–10 days for other countries. Express shipping is 2–3 days, and Priority is overnight to next-day for most major markets. Visit our Shipping & Delivery page for detailed rates.
Do you ship to my country?
We currently deliver to 45+ countries including the US, UK, Canada, Nigeria, Germany, France, Ireland, Netherlands, Australia, and more. If your country isn't listed at checkout, contact us — we may be able to arrange delivery.
How do I get free shipping?
Orders over $75 automatically qualify for free standard shipping to all supported countries. No promo code needed — the discount applies at checkout. Weight surcharges (+$2/lb over 5 lbs) may still apply for heavier orders.
Products
Are your products authentic Nigerian ingredients?
Absolutely. We source directly from trusted farms and suppliers across Nigeria. Every ingredient is genuine — no substitutes or imitations. Our quality team verifies every batch before it enters our warehouse.
What's included in a meal kit?
Each meal kit contains pre-measured, authentic ingredients plus a step-by-step recipe card. For example, our Jollof Rice Kit includes premium long-grain rice, signature spice blend, tomato paste mix, and seasonings — everything except fresh proteins, which you can add from your local store.
How should I store the ingredients?
Storage instructions are included with every order. Generally, spices and dried goods should be stored in a cool, dry place. Palm oil and specialty items may require refrigeration. All products have a shelf life of 6–12 months when stored properly.
Returns & Refunds
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order — whether due to quality issues, damaged items, or wrong products — contact us for a full refund or replacement. See our Return Policy page for complete details.
How long do refunds take?
Once your return is approved, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. The refund will appear on your original payment method. For store credit, it's issued immediately and can be used on your next order.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with anything.
